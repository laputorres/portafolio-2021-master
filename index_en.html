Process

1. Talk

Knowing all the details of how your site is required will be the first step to obtain a good result.

2. Planning

Each of the stages of the project is planned, as well as the times for the first advances and final delivery.

3. Design

Once the page design is defined, design samples are prepared, the first preview is delivered and the necessary adjustments are made.

4. Layout

It consists of taking the design and architecture of a website and converting it into code.

5. Programming

The programming of the web page is done, with the requirements of the project, during this stage the functionality of the site is tested.

6. The website is ready

The final delivery of the website is done once the client gives his approval, where support or advice is provided.
